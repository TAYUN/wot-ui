# Design Values

## Where does it come from

To adapt to the complex design and development tasks brought by cross-platform publishing for enterprise-level B-end mobile terminals, and to meet the stable expansion of B-end enterprise businesses and mobile operation characteristics, we have established a set of actionable design values to improve product experience consistency and the convenience of development and design invocation.

## A brief talk on values

Based on this background, wot's values can be divided into four parts: Unity, Pragmatism, Openness, and Friendliness. These values are interlocked, taking cross-platform unity as the foundation, pragmatic efficiency as the core, stable extensibility as the architecture, hot-switching of themes as the experience, and engineering friendliness as the collaboration standard, to create an enterprise-level uni mobile general component system.

### Unity

One set of components, consistent behavior across all platforms, smoothing out platform differences internally, providing completely unified APIs, interactions, styles, and events externally, strictly following design tokens, having clear and consistent icon rules, and conveying information stably and intuitively.

### Pragmatism

Provides an efficient and concise interface without redundant functions and decorations, with controllable density and strong reusability.

### Openness

Components are open but not out of control, flexible but not chaotic. Adopting a four-layer controllable architecture: atomic layer - semantic layer - component configuration layer - business extension layer; gradually increasing the definable scope, suitable for multi-business line collaboration, sharing the bottom layer and customizing the upper layer respectively.

### Friendliness

In line with friendly user experience and user-friendliness, deeply adapted to uni-app engineering, out-of-the-box for design and front-end teams, easy to maintain, and collaborative.
